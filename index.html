We believe that being a competent programmer is a journey.

College freshers have a habit of getting overwhelmed and confused as they get more and more assignments which take more and more of their time. They end up creating more backlogs and stress themselves out to the point where they aren't able to go exploring Computer Science on their own, enough to be more competent of a creator with code.

This is what we want to fix.

We will take you through a one month journey designed precisely for where you are in your journey and team you enough development with Python and other technologies in CS so that you can venture out on your own after the program ends.

We will teach you just as much that you can take in one month.

As stated earlier, This journey is supposed to be cattered to where you are in the journey. We won't want to overwhelm you to the point where you would have ended up learning nothing. But we will make sure that you get out of your comfort zone. Don't worry. We know what we are doing!
